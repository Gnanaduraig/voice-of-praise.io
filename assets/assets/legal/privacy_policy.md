# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: April 24, 2025_

Voice of Praise (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile, web, desktop (macOS/Windows), and iOS applications (collectively, the “App”).

## 1. Information We Collect

1. **Account Information:** When you sign in with Google, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture.
2. **User-Generated Content:** Posts, comments, referral codes, and profile details you create in the App.
3. **Device & Usage Data:** App version, device identifiers, and usage analytics (via Google Analytics or similar services).
4. **Preferences:** License acceptance, referral codes, and profile completion flags stored locally and in Firestore.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- **Authentication:** Validate your identity via Google Sign-In.
- **Personalization:** Display your profile, posts, and referral history.
- **Analytics & Performance:** Improve app stability, features, and overall user experience.
- **Notifications & Support:** Contact you about updates, features, or account issues.

## 3. Data Storage & Security

- We store user profiles and consent flags in Firebase Firestore under `users/{uid}` documents.
- Sensitive credentials (access tokens) are stored securely using the device’s Keychain/Keystore via `flutter_secure_storage`.
- All network communications are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.

## 4. Third-Party Services

We may share limited personal data with third parties for the following purposes:

- **Google Sign-In:** For authentication only; we do not share your data beyond verification.
- **Firebase:** Hosting, database, and analytics services.
- **Google Ads:** Display ads in compliance with our privacy choices; you can opt out via consent settings.

## 5. Your Rights & Choices

- You can revoke license acceptance by clearing the App’s local data or updating your Firestore record.
- You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us (see Section 9).

## 6. Children’s Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information of a child under 13, we will delete it.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy for legal, technical, or business reasons. We will notify you within the App or by email if major changes occur.
